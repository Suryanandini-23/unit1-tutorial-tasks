NRI Institute of Technology

About the College

NRI Institute of Technology (NRIIT) is one of the leading engineering colleges in Andhra Pradesh. The institution provides quality education, modern infrastructure, and opportunities for students to develop technical and professional skills.

Vision

To emerge as a center of excellence in technical education, research, and innovation, producing globally competent professionals with ethical values.

Mission

  • Provide quality education through effective teaching and learning.
  • Promote research, innovation, and entrepreneurship.
  • Develop leadership, communication, and professional skills.
  • Encourage ethical values and social responsibility.

Departments

  • Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
  • Information Technology (IT)
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)
  • Mechanical Engineering (ME)
  • Civil Engineering (CE)
  • Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (AI&DS)
